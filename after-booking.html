Before Your Appointment

  • Arrive with a clean, makeup-free face.
  • Moisturize your skin as usual, but avoid using sunscreen or oily products on the day of your appointment.
  • If you have allergies or sensitivities, please inform me in advance.
  • Bring inspiration photos if you have a specific look in mind.
  • For bridal or group services, confirm the location and time at least 48 hours before the event.

Payment & Policies

  • A deposit is required to secure your spot. The remaining balance is paid on the day of the service.
  • If you need to reschedule, please notify me at least 48 hours in advance.
  • Deposits are non-refundable but can be used for future services if you reschedule in time.
  • Accepted payment methods: bank transfer, cash, or deposit. Request an invoice if needed.

On the Day of Your Appointment

  • Be punctual to make the most of your session.
  • Wear comfortable clothing that is easy to remove (for events or weddings).
  • For home service, prepare a well-lit space with a table and chair.
  • False lashes are included. Let me know if you prefer not to use them.
