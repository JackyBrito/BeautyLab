Tips

How to make your makeup last longer?

Prep your skin well: cleansing, moisturizing, and primer. Use long-lasting products and set with powder and setting spray. Don't forget to touch up lips and T-zone!

Tricks for a natural and radiant look

Less is more: use light foundations, concealer only where needed, and cream blush for a fresh effect. Highlight key points and blend products well.

How to prevent eyeliner from smudging?

First apply an eyelid primer, use waterproof eyeliner, and set with eyeshadow of the same shade. Touch up only if needed throughout the day.
