FAQ

Frequently asked questions about services, booking, and policies.

I recommend booking your appointment at least 2 weeks before the event to ensure availability, especially during peak season (weddings, graduations, and parties). If you have a special date, book as soon as possible!

What if it's last minute? If you need urgent service, contact me via WhatsApp or Instagram. I'll always do my best to accommodate your schedule, even on short notice. Remember, Saturdays and Sundays fill up quickly.

Tip: For brides, it's best to book 2-3 months in advance to secure your trial and event day.

I use professional products from renowned brands like MAC, NARS, Urban Decay, Too Faced, Kryolan, and Anastasia Beverly Hills, among others. All products are long-lasting, sweat and tear resistant, and suitable for all skin types, including sensitive skin.

Hygiene and safety: Every brush and tool is disinfected before each client. I use hypoallergenic products and always ask about allergies or preferences.

Personalization: Makeup is adapted to your skin type, tone, and desired style, for a natural, elegant, or glam finish according to your event.

Yes, I offer home service in Mexico City and nearby areas (ask for coverage). The additional cost depends on the distance and area, and is quoted when booking.

What does home service include? I bring all the professional materials needed: lights, brushes, and products. I only need a comfortable space, chairs, mirrors, and good natural lighting.

Ideal for: Weddings, photoshoots, events at home, hotels, or venues. Just relax and enjoy the experience!

Social makeup: Between 60 and 90 minutes, depending on the look, skin type, and if you want additional hairstyling.
Brides: Up to 2 hours, as it includes a prior trial, skin preparation, premium lashes, and final touch-ups.
Self-makeup classes: Approximately 2 hours, with time for practice, questions, and personalized advice.

Tip: Arrive with a clean, makeup-free face to make the most of your session.

Classes are 100% personalized and practical. You'll learn techniques adapted to your face type, product selection, tools, skin preparation, concealing imperfections, foundation application, contouring, eyes, lips, and setting.

Includes:
  • Diagnosis of your skin type and product recommendations.
  • Review and advice on your own makeup kit.
  • Digital support material with tips and key steps.
  • Step-by-step guided practice and Q&A.
Goal: That you feel confident and able to create looks for your daily life or special events.

Yes, all social and bridal makeup services include the application of high-quality false lashes, adapted to your style and comfort.

Can I choose the type of lashes? Of course! I have natural, dramatic, and premium options. If you prefer not to use them, just let me know.

Tip: False lashes enhance your eyes and make your makeup look more professional in photos and events.

You can book your appointment through the Book menu, WhatsApp, or Instagram. To secure your spot, a deposit is required via bank transfer or deposit, and the rest is paid on the day of the service.

Accepted payment methods: Bank transfer, cash, and deposits. If you need an invoice, let me know when booking.

Tip: Book in advance to choose the time that suits you best and secure your spot, especially on weekends.

If you need to cancel or change your appointment date, please notify me at least 48 hours in advance to reschedule at no cost. The deposit is non-refundable in case of cancellation; however, you can use it for future services.

Why is a deposit required? The deposit secures your spot and allows efficient scheduling. If something comes up, I always look for options to reschedule your appointment.

Tip: If you have questions about the cancellation policy, feel free to contact me before booking.

Yes, I do makeup for groups for weddings, graduations, photoshoots, and special events.

How does it work? You can request a personalized quote for groups, family packages, or friends. The service is adapted to the number of people, schedules, and specific needs.

Tip: Booking as a group can have special benefits and discounts. Ask about current promotions!
